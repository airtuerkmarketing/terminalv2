## 1. Executive Overview

### Identity
airtuerk Service GmbH is a leading **B2B flight consolidator** (airline ticket wholesaler), **travel-technology provider**, and **global tourism connectivity specialist** founded in 2006 and headquartered in Frankfurt am Main, Germany. airtuerk operates at the technical and commercial intersection between supply (airlines, hotel suppliers, ground-service providers) and demand (travel agencies, tour operators, OTAs, and airlines), supplying airline tickets from approximately 170 airline partners alongside cross-market travel products. As part of the Berlin-based AERTiCKET Group, airtuerk combines global purchasing power with localized service expertise. The Managing Director is Ümit Tenekeci.

airtuerk is strictly **business-to-business**: it is not a consumer-facing travel agency, and end customers are serviced exclusively through partner channels.

### What a Flight Consolidator Does
A flight consolidator is a B2B wholesaler of flight tickets. airtuerk negotiates exclusive net and special fares directly with airlines, bundles them into a broad portfolio, and distributes them to travel-trade partners. This gives partners access to fares and inventory that would otherwise require their own IATA licence and ticketing capability. airtuerk also assumes the ticketing process and its associated liability on behalf of its partners.

### Core Value Proposition
airtuerk delivers a **technical all-in-one B2B ecosystem** summarized as "Special content. Global reach. One API," built on three pillars:

- **Exclusive flight content.** Net, special, and private fares negotiated directly with airlines and through the AERTiCKET Group's worldwide fare agreements, spanning 80,000+ global O&Ds with strong seasonal availability for leading holiday markets (Türkiye, Egypt, Greece).
- **Intuitive booking engines.** Purpose-built Internet Booking Engines (IBEs) that abstract GDS, NDC, and low-cost-carrier (LCC) complexity into a single workflow — usable by specialists and newcomers, with no prior GDS knowledge required.
- **Seamless integration and service.** An API-first technical backbone for system-to-system connectivity, supported by a multilingual service center operating 365 days a year.

airtuerk states it provides flight tickets, car hire, hotels, transfers, Rail&Fly, and package holidays via its own IBEs/Cache, XML, and APIs.

### Company Metrics
airtuerk reports approximately **2.5 million passengers (PAX) served per year** and **1,200+ active travel agencies** using its platforms daily across the DACH and Benelux regions, with growth reported each quarter. The Frankfurt entity has a team of roughly 60–65, within a group of around 110 people across all sites.

### Strategic Positioning & New Initiatives
airtuerk positions itself as the **connectivity layer** between travel supply and demand, emphasizing API-first product development over legacy GDS-only workflows, cross-market consolidation (flights, hotels, transfers, rail, rental cars) under a unified stack, and a brand-portfolio architecture in which specialized products address specific partner segments. Current strategic initiatives include:

- **ATBeds** — a newly established standalone bedbank / hotel-IBE company (see Sections 2 and 4).
- **airtuerk International** — a dedicated team driving international market expansion and global partner acquisition (see Section 4).
- **airtuerk Intelligence** — airtuerk's AI chat hub (the consumer of this knowledge base).

## 2. Core Products & IBE Ecosystem

airtuerk operates a connected ecosystem of Internet Booking Engines (IBEs) and management tools. All products share a common backend infrastructure and content pool, and partners access a unified portfolio of travel services that are bookable, requestable, or retrievable in a few clicks.

| Product | Type | Primary purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Cockpit | Multi-product IBE (flagship) | Central booking engine for worldwide flights plus rental cars, hotels & transfers in one interface |
| multicheck | IBE / price-comparison portal | Flight price research, real-time fare comparison, allotment access, SSR & PNR management |
| airtuerk Holidays | Package tool | Dynamic packaging and booking of tour-operator package holidays |
| airtuerkPRO | Mobile app | Mobile booking and booking management for partners |
| ATBeds | Bedbank / hotel-IBE | Worldwide hotel sourcing for agencies, delivered through Cockpit |
| myBooking | Management portal | Online check-in and post-booking management |
| rentalCar / Transfer / airLounge / Rail&Fly | Ancillary tools | Cross-market add-ons within the booking flow |

### Cockpit — "Our flagship IBE. Where bookings happen."
Cockpit is airtuerk's **central, advanced multi-product Internet Booking Engine**, providing worldwide access to nearly all flights plus rental cars, hotels, and transfers in a single interface. Cockpit offers a clear, user-friendly UX optimized for travel agencies, requiring no prior GDS knowledge, and provides exclusive B2B special and net fares for 175+ airlines. Cockpit supports a wide range of specialized fare types and ancillary functions, including:

- Fare families: **NET, TOP, LOW-COST, DIR, CHA, and VFR** fares, plus special fares.
- **NDC** flights and **SSR services** (seat selection, extra baggage, meals, plus requests such as wheelchair assistance and unaccompanied minors) plus lounge access.
- **Upselling** across all fare families with simple fare selection and transparent pricing.

Operational features include multi-port search, flexible ±3-day date queries with automatic display of alternative airports, "save for later" draft storage, automated info messages on rebooking or cancellation, in-system check-in, multiple payment methods, and a sell mode without deposit. Agencies can build their own itineraries, share information team-internally within the PNR, and freely define a fare-based service surcharge. Tickets can be booked, cancelled, or changed in a single click, with all transactions automatically documented and traceable through the backend.

### multicheck — "The Price Hunter"
multicheck is airtuerk's **all-in-one price-comparison portal** for touristic destinations. Its Smart Fare Engine compares **GDS, NDC, and airline direct connections in real time** and surfaces **airtuerk allotments** — favorable contingent prices for holiday destinations and specially selected routes. A visual price-and-date matrix supports fast consultation. multicheck provides price alarms and low-fare tracking, weekly search intervals that run unattended, and clear tariff management with immediate option visibility and rebooking possibilities. It supports reissue, refund, and void workflows and agent-level markup visibility. Built-in add-ons cover transfers, lounges, and cars; options can be pre-reserved or reactivated. multicheck is mobile-ready via airtuerkPRO.

### airtuerk Holidays — "The Package Builder"
airtuerk Holidays is airtuerk's **package-holiday and dynamic-packaging tool**. It offers packages by budget, duration, and destination from tour operators, and lets partners combine scheduled, charter, and low-cost flights with hotels and transfers. airtuerk Holidays provides direct access to tour-operator fares with attractive commissions and flexible pricing models (high margins for partners). It connects 1,000+ partner hotels worldwide and 30+ German tour operators directly, with no minimum-turnover commitment, central handling of cancellations and rebookings, a free reservation system, SEPA/invoice/credit-card payment, and Bistro/TOMA integration plus training programs.

### airtuerkPRO
airtuerkPRO is airtuerk's **mobile application** for partners, enabling fast, intuitive, mobile flight booking and booking management anywhere, anytime — including booking lookup and modification, notification handling (schedule changes, cancellations, ticket time limits), and on-the-go SSR and PNR queries. It is exclusive to airtuerk partners and serves as the mobile bundling point for airtuerk's tools, including multicheck.

### ATBeds — "Worldwide hotels. One booking engine. Built for agencies."
ATBeds is airtuerk's **new standalone bedbank and hotel-IBE**, established as an independent company within the wider airtuerk group and based in Istanbul (Akasya Tower). ATBeds is built on its **own hotel content and own technology**, and its dedicated hotel-IBE is delivered to airtuerk's travel-agency partners **through Cockpit** — so hotel bookings happen inside the system partners already use, with no separate login and no new system to learn. ATBeds extends the ecosystem from flights into accommodation and is launching through 2026; airtuerk's leadership describes the accompanying Bed Bank API as giving partners broad access to accommodation content. ATBeds positions airtuerk as a cross-product distributor rather than a flight-only consolidator, complementing airtuerk Holidays' tour-operator package inventory by broadening reach into global and independent hotel supply.

### myBooking
myBooking is airtuerk's **booking-management portal**, providing online check-in and post-booking management for partners and their customers.

### Ancillary Tools
airtuerk bundles ancillaries directly into the booking flow, with no third-party redirects:

- **rentalCar (Car rental)** — "The Extras That Complete the Journey." Cars from multiple providers with 24/7 support, premium insurance options, and card-free booking.
- **Transfer** — "The Seamless Connection." Airport transfers from arrival to hotel, booked directly within the booking flow.
- **airLounge** — lounge access added to bookings.
- **Rail&Fly** — rail connection to/from the airport as a complement to the flight.

## 3. API Connections & Technical Infrastructure

### API-First Architecture (100% In-House Technology)
airtuerk operates an **API-first architecture** built on 100% in-house technology: every booking, content, and management capability is exposed as a programmable endpoint rather than being locked inside a UI product. The airtuerk API is designed for any technology team that needs to embed professional-grade flight content into its own product — whether an OTA building a consumer booking flow, a tour operator packaging flights with accommodation, a B2B portal serving travel agents, or a booking engine requiring high-volume cache queries. airtuerk provides its own API, XML connectivity, a Cache, partner extranet portals, and mid-office synchronization, supporting fully automated, end-to-end workflows from offer to booking.

Core API capabilities exposed through the airtuerk API Gateway include:

- Multiple-airline combination and one-way + return content
- SSR booking function and online check-in function
- Cache data query (high-volume) and automated schedule-change handling
- B2B and B2C management tooling
- Automatic refund feature and integrated invoicing + credit-card payment
- Content controls for volume deals, route-specific special conditions, and luggage

## Glossary of Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| airtuerk | The company / brand; styled lowercase. |
| Flight consolidator | A B2B wholesaler that buys exclusive net/special airfares from airlines and resells them to the travel trade. |
| Bedbank | A B2B wholesaler of hotel/accommodation inventory contracted in bulk and resold to the travel trade (airtuerk's bedbank is ATBeds). |
| IBE | Internet Booking Engine — a web-based interface for booking flights and travel products. |
| SSR | Special Service Request — passenger-level service codes (e.g., seat, meal, baggage, wheelchair, unaccompanied minor). |
| PNR | Passenger Name Record — the booking record containing passenger and itinerary data. |
| O&D | Origin & Destination pair — a routing pair, e.g., FRA–IST, used to define and price air travel. |
| NDC | New Distribution Capability — IATA's XML-based airline content-distribution standard. |
| GDS | Global Distribution System — e.g., Amadeus, Sabre, Travelport. |
| LCC | Low-Cost Carrier. |
| Cache | A pre-aggregated store of fares/availability enabling fast, high-volume querying via the API. |
| Net / special / private fare | A contracted airfare (often below published prices) on which a reseller can apply its own markup. |
| Rail&Fly | A rail connection to/from the airport offered alongside a flight booking. |
| Flugdisposition | Flight disposition — the operational function managing flight content/allotments and related booking operations. |
| DACH | The German-speaking market region: Germany (D), Austria (A), Switzerland (CH). |
